# ChatHub - Your Adorable Chat Application

Welcome to **ChatHub**, a cute and colorful chat application where you can create rooms, chat with friends, and express yourself with fun emojis! The app has a beautiful design with smooth animations and a friendly user experience.

---

## What is ChatHub?

ChatHub is a web-based chat application that lets you create your own chat rooms and send messages. Whether you want to chat with friends, organize group conversations, or just have fun with a cute interface, ChatHub is here for you! The app works completely in your browser without needing any server – all your data is saved locally.

## How to Get Started

1. **Open the app** - Open \index.html\ in your web browser
2. **Create an account** - Click "Sign Up" and enter your email and password
3. **Login** - Use your email to login to your account
4. **Create a room** - Click "New Room" and give it a fun name and emoji
5. **Start chatting** - Click on a room and start sending messages
6. **Invite others** - Share the room code with friends so they can join

---

## How It Works

### Authentication System
When you sign up, your account information is stored safely in your browser. When you login, the app checks your email and password. Once logged in, all your rooms and messages are loaded from storage. If you close the website and come back later, just login again with the same email and password – everything will be exactly as you left it!

### Chat Rooms
Each room is like a separate conversation space. You can create multiple rooms for different groups of friends. When you join a room, you become a member and can see all the messages in that room. The app shows you which members are in each room so you know who you're chatting with.

### Messages & Timestamps
Every message is saved with information about who sent it and exactly when they sent it. This helps you follow the conversation flow and know when things happened. Messages load instantly when you open a room.

### Local Storage
The app uses your browser's storage to save all your data locally. This means:
- Your account information is kept safe
- Your rooms and messages stay saved
- Everything works offline (no internet needed after loading)
- Your data never leaves your computer
